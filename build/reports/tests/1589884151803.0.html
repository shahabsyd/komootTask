        Trains

        Travelling across Germany by train is easy with fromAtoB! We will take care of everything in order to find you the fastest or cheapest ticket for your rail journey: comparing the offers of different providers (e.g. Deutsche Bahn or Flixtrain) as well as understanding complex fare structures. You can then book your connection directly through us. One more advantage for you: bus and flight connections as well as carpooling offers will also be displayed in the search results if the option is selected. If you want to find out more about travelling by rail and train tickets in Germany, check out our Guide to Super Saver Fares.

        Buses

        Travelling by bus is THE new easy way to get around Germany and a popular alternative to rail travel. Meanwhile, we can hardly imagine our roads without those green Flixbus coaches. If travelling by bus is an option for you, fromAtoB is just what you need: it allows you to compare bus, train, flight and carpooling prices, including special offers. Simply enter your point of departure, destination and travel dates, then select your preferred mode(s) of transport and we will show you the cheapest and fastest offers. Everything you have to do is to book the ticket that suits you the most for your trip.

        Flights

        Flying is always a popular option for longer distances. fromAtoB will show you if booking a flight is a good option for your trip, compares the prices to rail and road travel and informs you about the fastest and the cheapest connections. Alternative modes of transport such as trains, buses and rideshares can also be included in your search, depending on the selected options. Once you have found your flight ticket, you can easily book through fromAtoB.

        Carpooling

        Spontaneous and last-minute bookings are made easy with carpooling, providing you with easy access to those smaller towns where cheap tickets (e.g. for trains or buses) otherwise are hard to come by. To find carpooling offers, select “Carpooling” as one of the transport options to search for. By adding this option, you may find a last-minute rideshare to your destination at an affordable price. You can also compare the prices and journey times to travelling by bus, train or plane.
